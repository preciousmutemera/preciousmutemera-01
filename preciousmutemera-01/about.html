About Us

Papila is one of the competitive pre-schools in our community registered under the ministry of primary education. We opened doors in 2010 with an overwhelming response and need from the community. We have children over 120 attending pre-school. Besides pre-school we also tend to offer remedial education, hot lunch and snacks, lending liabrary and mom and dad workshops.We have been blessed with Jungle Tots which is an established playgroup franchise that gives young children the opportunity to learn and have fun.


Our Mission

The mission for Papila pre-school is to educate children to become leaders. We do this through our commitment. Beginning in the classroom with new ways of understanding and exposure to new ideas


Our Vision

To provide a safe ,comfortable environment conducive for children to grow, develop and learn through excellence and professionalism.
